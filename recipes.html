Classic Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 apples, peeled and sliced

  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Pie crust (top and bottom)

Instructions:

  2. Mix apples with sugar, flour, and cinnamon
  3. Fill bottom crust with apple mixture

Banana Smoothie Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 3 frozen bananas
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • Granola for topping
  • Fresh berries

Instructions:

  1. Blend frozen bananas with yogurt and honey
  2. Pour into a bowl
  3. Top with granola and fresh berries
  4. Enjoy immediately!

Strawberry Shortcake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries, sliced
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Biscuits or shortcake
  • Whipped cream
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. Mix strawberries with sugar and let sit for 30 minutes
  2. Split biscuits in half
  3. Layer with strawberries and whipped cream
  4. Serve immediately

Orange Citrus Salad

Ingredients:

  • 4 oranges, peeled and sectioned
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Arrange orange sections in a bowl
  2. Drizzle with honey and lime juice
  3. Garnish with mint and nuts
  4. Chill for 30 minutes before serving
